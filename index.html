Healthcare Analytics · Experiential Project

Healthcare No-Show Forecasting & AI Decision Support

SmartHealth AI × Northeastern University · INT5964 · Sponsor: Trang Bui · April 2026 — June 2026

A forecasting-driven analytics engagement built to answer a real business question for a healthcare-tech sponsor: which patients are statistically likely to miss their appointment, and what would it take for AI to fix that at scale? Combined probability-based prediction models with market and academic research to make the case for where AI adoption creates the most value for clinics.

73,811Appointments Analyzed
16.8%No-Show Rate Modeled
0.86Best Model AUC
Built a forecasting and probability-based prediction pipeline across 73,811+ patient appointments, synthesizing 4 peer-reviewed sources and 150+ scheduling studies.
Designed a 3-category clinic segmentation framework (General Outpatient, Virtual Care, Specialized Diagnostic) for tuned per-segment forecasting.
Identified and statistically validated the top 5 predictors of patient no-shows: lead time, prior history, visit type, insurance status, contact availability.
Benchmarked 3 classification models and translated the best-performing model into a business case for AI-driven scheduling.
Presented a 15+ page analytical report and executive presentation to a real industry sponsor.
